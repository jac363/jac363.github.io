Nice to meet you.

I'm a 22-year old artist who enjoys painting with gouache and watercolor and loves sharing my art with others. I've loved art all my life. During the COVID pandemic, I was studying ornithology in preparation for Science Olympiad, and I kept sane by painting birds. I fell in love with watercolor and gouache as they are perfect mediums for urban sketching.

I try to keep my commissions very affordable. In the digital age, especially given the prevalence of AI art, human-made artwork becomes all the more special, and I've found that people really cherish being gifted artwork. Thanks for checking out my work!
